80s Basketball

History of 80s Basketball

1. “Showtime” Lakers: Exciting and successful team led by Magic Johnson and Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, won five NBA championships in the 1980s. 2. Michael Jordan’s Emergence: Rising star in the 1980s, Jordan’s athleticism and scoring prowess made him a fan favorite. 3. “Bad Boys” Pistons: Detroit Pistons known for their physical play, won back-to-back NBA championships in 1989 and 1990 with tough defense and intense rivalry with the Chicago Bulls.
